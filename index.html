About Us

Resonance Academy is the sister concern of Resonance Studios, a leading audio production studio, based in Bengaluru and Mumbai. Ram Prashant (Founder & Managing Director) started Resonance Studios in 2005, after finishing his degree in Sound Engineering at SAE. Over the years he has visited a number of recording studios and audio schools abroad, attended seminars, and worked with artists around the globe. Resonance Studios provides high quality audio production services for both artists and corporates, and has been a pioneer in the audio industry for 10 years (and counting). At present, Resonance has two full-fledged recording studios, an audio academy, and a Jam room.


Resonance Studios is also linked with Qyuki, a digital media company located in Mumbai, which was founded by A. R. Rahman & Shekhar Kapoor. Resonance Academy has been one of the pioneers of inculcating Dj courses in Bengaluru is one of the best DJ, Music Production, and Recording schools in the city. It has churned out over 200 students who have performed at music festivals like Supersonic and played at venues like Pebble, Blue Frog, and Church Street Social, to name a few. We offer courses that are tailor made to suit the needs of each individual who aspires to reach their goals in terms of audio.


At Resonance, the students are trained on state-of- the-art hardware and software used in the audio industry all over the world. Students are instructed by a dedicated team of experienced musicians, DJs, and audio engineers who use a hands-on approach and encourage them to participate in live projects at the studio. Our long-term courses also include studio time for students to record, mix, and master their own audio projects. When students pass out we help them to get gigs at popular venues. Electronic Music Production

Key Facts

Duration:

2 Months, Maximum 4-6 Students per batch, 2 classes per week. 2 hours per class. Weekend batches mainly. Flexible for weekday batches also. Ideal for college students, working individuals and not to disrupt their weekday schedule.

Objective:

Learn how to produce/compose music using digital tools & software.
Main course module based on the DAW: ABLETON LIVE

Overview

The Electronic Music Production course is a pathway for passionate enthusiasts who have always had the dream of producing music with a signature touch. We make this dream a reality by guiding you with the best of techniques and concepts that will make your workflow faster and better. There is a strong emphasis laid on the concept of learning by doing. As a result, this aids you in refining your creative expression and output as a music producer and a performer.

Why this course works for you?

  • Learning the core fundamentals of music theory (sub topics include Musical chords, music scales, song structure, melody and basslines)
  • Learning about the interface and workflow of the software : ABLETON LIVE (used by popular artists such as skrillex, Flume, Junkie XL, Daft Punk, Armin van buuren etc)
  • Learn about MIDI and its various concepts
  • Learning about popular VST instruments and FX plugins
  • Creating professional quality music with the help of beats, loops and samples
  • Learning to enhance the sonic quality and tone of your song composition with help of compression and EQ plugins
  • Working with synthesizers and understanding various concepts of audio synthesis
  • Learning to use state of the art equipment and budget home studio devices
  • Learn fundamental concepts of Remixing
  • Mixing tips and tricks and a brief session on mastering

A Give And Take Philosophy

At Resonance we give students the ability to write music early into the course. They learn to edit audio and create re-mixes right off the bat. We build a strong foundation for their music with emphasis on structure and style. We help them sculpt raw sounds and create a musical signature and identity. They will learn to perfect their music with clean mixes . This EMP Course is facilitated by a faculty renowned in the Indian EDM circuit, Mr. Guru Prasad who has his own record label titled "Since 16 Music". His musical work has been curated and supported by World renowned Dj Sander Van Doom and has gotten airplay by the resident DJs of the "Ministry of Sound" Collective We limit each batch to a maximum of 6. Small sized batches will ensure clear communication, interaction and feedback between the teacher and the student. This also allows for a casual yet focused environment that is most conducive for learning.  We also believe in the philosophy that "IMPLEMENTED Knowledge is power". So therefore, we strongly encourage you to follow up whatever is taught in class back at home so that, in essence, brings about the true potential of learning by doing.

Audio/Sound Engineering

Key Facts

Duration:

1 Year, 4-6 Students per batch. 3 classes per week. 2 hours per class. Weekday batches mainly

Objective:

To create an environment that helps in providing a knowledge base that would help one to understand the finer nuances of sound design.
Refine and perfect the art of mixing and mastering which would be an invaluable asset for both a studio engineer as well as a live sound mix engineer

Overview

The Audio Engineering Program is meant for those who truly want to explore the possibilities of working in a studio environment, processing and polishing sound to cater to the ever-growing media industry. The importance of an audio engineer is reflected in a lot of career-oriented sectors like radio, TV, advertising, film, live concert mix- engineers and many more.

Why this course works for you?

  • Introduction into the world of sound and audio (which includes audio fundamentals, human hearing and psychoacoustics, decibels etc.)
  • Learn in-depth knowledge of Studio equipment (Such as microphones and miking techniques, audio cables and peripherals, signal flow, audio interfaces, dynamic signal processing and effects)
  • Sharpen your listening skills with ear training techniques and further enhance the experience with modules on acoustics, surround sound, monitoring and amplification
  • Learn about the ever changing landscape of the music industry with a module on music business
  • Learn about music theory to bring out the musical and creative expressions of your artistic personality
  • Dive deep into the world of Digital Music Production with comprehensive modules on Computer fundamentals, MIDI, Synthesis and sampling, and finally programming and sequencing music using Apple's principal music software LOGIC PRO X help of compression and EQ plugins
  • Learning about digital audio mediums and universally accepted audio formats
  • A brief look into the aspect of Audio POST PRODUCTION and Live sound.(Sub topics include post production techniques, on location and foley recording, live sound system design, broadcasting setups)
  • A very detailed course module on advanced mixing and mastering.

A Give And Take Philosophy

At Resonance Academy, we provide you with industry-standard facilities teaching you the pros and cons of the trade and help you deliver creative services to a world full of opportunities. During the program, students will learn to create, edit, mix and master a wide range of musical styles. They will learn about a number of styles of music as well as how to produce music. We also introduce students to the art of recording and mixing music in studio sessions. This Audio engineering Course is facilitated by a certified "Apple" trainer, who has an active membership with global audio entities such as AES and SMPTE(Society of Motion Picture & Television Engineers) Being adept at all the major audio/acoustic scenarios, the faculty also facilitates external visits to locations other than the studio which would make for real-world scenarios of audio production. Small sized batches will ensure clear communication and interaction between the teacher and the student. This also allows for a casual yet focused environment that is most conducive for learning.

DJ Elemental Course

Key Facts

Duration:

2 Months, 4-6 Students per batch, 2-3 classes per week. 2 hours per class. Weekend batches mainly. Flexible for weekday batches also ideal for college students, working individuals and not to disrupt their weekday schedule

Objective:

To build a strong foundation in basic DJ skills, beat matching, etc. and getting acquainted and comfortable with Pioneer CDJ and DJM systems practice time for your DJ sets at the studio.

Overview

The DJ Elemental Program is an 8 week course for music enthusiasts who will experience a new way to mix and perform as a hobby or as a stepping stone into the DJing world.

Why this course works for you?

  • Introduction into the world of DJing
  • A comprehensive look into Pioneer CDJ 2000 nexus players and Pioneer DJM 900 nexus mixer
  • Detailed study of the DJ system (terminologies, calibration techniques etc.)
  • Learning the fundamentals and techniques of Equalizing using the equalizer
  • Ear training practices (How to listen to music and how to do beat-matching)
  • Learning to use Rekordbox(Software) for efficient organization of your music and planning your set
  • Learning to use the DJM’s built-in effects unit
  • A Brief look into Digital DJing: Learning to record a live DJ set in Ableton Live (Software) and a brief look at the Traktor Scratch Pro DJ (Software)
  • Assessment of your knowledge with a final evaluation and exam

KL Rahul

Guru Prasad

Guru Prasad is an electronic music producer and DJ from Bangalore, India. Guru hails from the once humble traditional getups of Malleshwaram and has explored the world of futuristic, modern, progressive sounds and musical elements; a paradox in itself. He has released music under various labels, and used to produce and perform under his elusive alias; "iced Monkey". He learned DJing at ILM academy in 2008.His music is a confluence of Tech house with futuristic sounds and aggressive elements. His compositions have been curated and supported by the likes of global DJs, music entities and podcasts such as 'Sander Van Doorn', Kiss FM, 'Ministry of sound' collective, 'StromKraft' Radio and local podcast " Tenzi FM". Having almost performed literally everywhere in Bangalore, he's also well-versed with the DAW (Ableton live), Guru has been a much sought-after guest faculty by several prominent music venues and studios. He currently performs under the artists name "sixth Tone".

Some of his accolades include:
  • His music tracks being breaking through to the Beatport top 100 techno/minimal charts
